Delete Your Account

At Wildslate, we respect your privacy and your right to control your data. This page explains how you can request deletion of your account and associated data.

How to Delete Your Account

You can request account deletion using one of the following methods:

  • Inside the app: Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account
  • Or send a request via email (see below)
Email Request:
Send an email to:
himanshu.newlife@gmail.com

Include:
  • Your registered email address
  • Your username (if available)
  • Subject: Account Deletion Request

What Happens When You Delete Your Account

  • Your profile and personal data will be permanently deleted
  • Your messages, media, and content may be removed
  • Your subscriptions and purchases will be cancelled
  • Your blogs, products, and listings will be deleted
⚠️ Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

Data Retention

Some data may be retained for a limited period if required for:

  • Legal compliance
  • Fraud prevention
  • Financial record keeping

Processing Time

We process deletion requests within 7–15 business days.

Your Rights (GDPR & Global Users)

You have the right to request deletion of your personal data (“Right to be Forgotten”) under applicable laws such as GDPR.
