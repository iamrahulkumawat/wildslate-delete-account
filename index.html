Delete Your Wildslate Account

At Wildslate, we respect your privacy and your control over your data. You can request account deletion at any time using the methods below.

Delete Directly from the App

You can easily delete your account inside the Wildslate app:

  • Open the Wildslate App
  • Go to Settings
  • Navigate to Account
  • Tap on Delete Account
  • Confirm your request

Delete via Email Request

If you cannot access your account, send a request to:

Email:
himanshu.newlife@gmail.com

Include the following details:

  • Your registered email address
  • Your username (if available)
  • Subject: Account Deletion Request

What Happens After Deletion

  • Your account and profile data will be permanently deleted
  • Your messages, media, and shared content may be removed
  • Your subscriptions will be cancelled
  • Your products, blogs, and listings will be deleted
⚠️ Warning: This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

Data Retention

Certain data may be retained temporarily where required for legal, financial, or security purposes.

Processing Time

Deletion requests are typically processed within 7–15 business days.

Your Rights

You have the right to request deletion of your personal data under applicable laws including GDPR (“Right to be Forgotten”).
